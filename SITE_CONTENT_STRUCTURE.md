## 3. Product Catalog Data Structure
Every product featured on the site follows a standardized B2B data schema:

*   **Identification**: Product Name and Category.
*   **Short Description**: High-impact marketing hook.
*   **Full Description**: Multi-paragraph technical deep-dive (Average 300-500 words).
*   **Specs Table**: Dimensions, Material grade, IP/IK ratings, Certification status.
*   **Features List**: Technical advantages (e.g., UV-stable, self-extinguishing).
*   **Media**: High-resolution gallery and interactive 3D GLB models.
*   **Downloads**: CAD drawings, Technical broshures, and site-prep guides.
