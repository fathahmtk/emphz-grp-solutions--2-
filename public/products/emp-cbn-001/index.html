Security Cabins

Sentinel Guard Kiosk

Security cabins are manufactured using GRP panels and structural components for long-term outdoor use. These cabins are commonly installed at factory gates, residential complexes, and infrastructure sites.

Why It Exists

Provides an insulated, rust-free alternative to GI sheet cabins that leak or get too hot.

Typical Usage

Factory gates, residential complexes, and infrastructure construction points.

Recommended When

  • High rainfall areas requiring leak-proof roofs
  • Long-term deployment at site gates
  • Projects requiring AC-ready design

Features

  • Leak proof roof
  • Resists heat
  • Easy to install
