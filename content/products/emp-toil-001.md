GRP portable toilet units are factory-manufactured cabins used in construction sites, public places, events, and institutions. GRP construction helps avoid corrosion and water damage common in steel toilets.

## Why It Exists
Avoids corrosion and water damage common in steel toilets in high-use or outdoor locations.

## Typical Usage
Labor camps, construction sites, public grounds, and temporary institutions.

## Recommended When
- Sites requiring frequent cleaning with water
- Coastal areas prone to rusting
- Temporary installations needing quick deployment

## Features
- Strong GRP shell
- Easy to wash
- Does not absorb odors
