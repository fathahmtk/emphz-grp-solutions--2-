The S2 Sunshade protects sensitive field instruments from direct sunlight, rain, and physical impact. It prevents overheating of electronics and ensures accurate readings in harsh climates.

## Why It Exists
Prevents "LCD blackening" and overheating of transmitters in direct desert or tropical sun.

## Typical Usage
Oil & Gas fields, power plants, and outdoor process industries.

## Recommended When
- Protecting pressure transmitters
- Outdoor gauge protection
- High UV exposure zones

## Features
- UV blocking
- Chemical resistant
- Lightweight
