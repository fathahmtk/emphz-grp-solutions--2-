UrbanCell RETAIL is a factory-built, plug-in infrastructure cell designed for rapid deployment as retail hubs, mini-markets, or convenience store setups. Built with high-durability GRP, it features glass facades and integrated thermal management for modern urban commerce.

## Why It Exists
Traditional brick-and-mortar retail takes months to build; UrbanCell deploys in hours with a premium aesthetic.

## Typical Usage
Mini-markets, convenience store setups, pop-up shops, and urban hubs.

## Recommended When
- Smart city projects requiring fast ROI
- Areas with limited construction footprints
- High-traffic urban zones needing premium design

## Features
- Plug-and-play electrical
- Integrated POS ready
- Smart locks
- Self-cleaning coating
