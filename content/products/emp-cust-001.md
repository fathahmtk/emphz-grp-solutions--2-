EMPHZ undertakes custom GRP fabrication based on client drawings or jointly developed designs. Products are manufactured after drawing approval and specification finalization.

## Why It Exists
When standard products do not meet unique site dimensions or structural requirements.

## Typical Usage
Custom enclosures, special-purpose cabins, GRP rooms, covers, and project-specific components.

## Recommended When
- Unique dimensions required
- Special resin requirements (Acid resistance)
- Joint engineering development needed

## Features
- Custom design
- Material choice
- Precision molding
