The RedGuard series is engineered to protect vital fire safety equipment from the elements. Unlike steel cabinets that rust shut, our GRP hinges and bodies ensure the equipment is accessible when it matters most.

## Why It Exists
To ensure fire safety equipment is not compromised by rusted cabinet doors in outdoor settings.

## Typical Usage
Refineries, outdoor industrial plants, and marine jetties.

## Recommended When
- Outdoor fire hydrant points
- Marine and offshore platforms
- Chemical plants with corrosive atmosphere

## Features
- High-visibility finish
- Corrosion-proof hinges
- Weather sealed
