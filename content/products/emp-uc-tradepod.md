TradePod™ is a high-spec modular showcase designed for temporary exhibitions, product launches, or semi-permanent dealership extensions. It offers a premium glass-heavy aesthetic that mirrors high-end corporate interiors.

## Why It Exists
To provide a mobile, high-margin rental platform for brands requiring instant physical presence.

## Typical Usage
Trade shows, automotive showrooms, and luxury brand launches.

## Recommended When
- Product launch campaign
- Exhibition presence
- Semi-permanent dealership extensions

## Features
- Panoramic glass walls
- Designer interior finish
- Acoustic treatment
- Fast 4-hour setup
