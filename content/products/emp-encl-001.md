GRP electrical enclosures manufactured by EMPHZ are used to house electrical and electronic equipment in outdoor and indoor locations. These enclosures are commonly selected where steel cabinets corrode or require frequent maintenance.

## Why It Exists
Practical replacement for steel where corrosion, repainting, and maintenance become recurring problems.

## Typical Usage
Typical applications include electrical distribution panels, junction boxes, metering enclosures, solar inverter housings, and telecom equipment cabinets.

## Recommended When
- Outdoor installations
- Coastal and humid regions
- Industrial environments

## Features
- No rust or corrosion
- No repainting required
- Non-conductive body
- UV stable outer finish
