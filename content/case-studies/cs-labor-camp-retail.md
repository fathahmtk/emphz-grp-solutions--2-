## Challenge
A labor camp housing 5,000 residents lacked dignified retail infrastructure, leading to disorganized street vending and hygiene concerns.

## Solution
Designed and installed a custom **CampMart™** retail block featuring **Eco-San GRP Toilet Cabins** and **StoreBox™** inventory modules.

## Outcome
Standardized retail availability for residents. Improved sanitation metrics. Secured POS integration reduced cash-handling risks for camp operators.
